Digital Bottles

I run a great website, DigitalBottles.com, with Ali Eslami. It brings the experience of sending a message in a bottle into the 21st century.

Sometimes you just want to be heard. Sometimes, you feel like you need to dispense a nugget of wisdom to the world. And sometimes, you're simply in the mood for a conversation!

Digital bottles lets you anonymously write your heart out and send your message out into the digital ocean. Messages are received by random users who can then reply.
