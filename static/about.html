About me

I am currently the Data Science Lead at Hg Capital. Sitting within the Portfolio team, supporting boards and senior management on key value creation topics, my key focus area is the application of Analytics and AI methods to drive enterprise value across the lifecycle of a deal from diligence to exit. Topics span long-term data enablement strategy, to more tactical commercial projects, to broader operational support.

Previously I was a Data Scientist at the Boston Consulting Group in the GAMMA Analytics Team. Before that I was Data Analyst at OC&C Strategy Consultants, and a Lead Data Scientist at VisualDNA.

Previously, I was a DPhil student in the Department of Engineering Science at the University of Oxford, where I was a member of the Visual Geometry Group under the supervision of Prof Andrew Zisserman. Before that, I received my Masters degree in Engineering Science, also from the University of Oxford.

I am one of the longest serving members of the Magdalen College pool and football teams, and have captained both during my time at Magdalen. My (brief) football biography is here.
